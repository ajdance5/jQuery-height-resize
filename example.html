One Hour Plus

Not all podcasts are created in equal measures, when you want something to settle into, try these top 5 podcasts of mine that regulary run at 60 minutes plus

  1. Dan Carlin's Hardcore History
  2. Longform
  3. Bullseye With Jesse
  4. This American Life
  5. Invisibilia

Easy Listening

Podcast are the perfect format to listen to while doing other tasks, but that also means your attention can be divided. My top 5 podcasts for times you might not be able to absorb 100% of what you hear.

  1. Desert Island Discs
  2. The Worst Idea of All Time
  3. Song Exploder
  4. Mystery Show
  5. Mortified

Brain Food

Like to stretch your knowledge when you listen. Try these top 5 to enrich your mind and push the boundaries of what you know

  1. The Infinite Monkey Cage
  2. Radiolab
  3. Lexicon Valley
  4. Hidden Brain
  5. What's the Point

Funny

It's a risk with podcasts as you could burst out laughing at inopportune moments but here are my top 5 podcasts for the laughs

  1. The Worst Idea of All Time
  2. Bill Burr's Monday Morning Podcast
  3. Junktime
  4. The Sporkful
  5. TOFOP

In The Car

Could otherwise be described as podcasts that require your full attention, if you spend a lot of time in the car here are my top 5 to check out.

  1. Dan Carlin's Hardcore History
  2. Inquiring Minds
  3. Better Off Dead
  4. BBC World Documentaries
  5. What's The Point

Part of A Series

Many podcasts come not just as episodes of a programme but a cohesive part of a singular project. Here are my top 5

  1. Better Off Dead
  2. The Season
  3. Serial - Season One
  4. Welcome To Nightvale
  5. They're Alive
